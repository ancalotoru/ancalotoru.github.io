FiabiliApp

Discovering fake-news

FiabiliApp is a website that discovers the fake-news. It works leading with 4 indicators.

  • Counts the number of re-tweets and favorites of the new tweeted by the publisher.
  • Calculates a newspaper impact factor depending on the number of everyday readers.
  • Measures the novelty of the new looking for the same new in other places.
  • Uses a Naïve Bayes classification algorithm to predict the fakeness with certain probability.

After all, it weighs up a global index called FiabiliScore which tells the reader in percentaje the fakeness of the evaluated new.

This work was computed by Naïve News team created on Hack4Good 2017
